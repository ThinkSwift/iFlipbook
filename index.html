Draw fast. Frame it. Animate it in seconds.

A simple creative loop that scales with you.

iFlipbook is designed for fast experimentation at the start and deeper creative control as your work becomes more sophisticated.

  • 1 Sketch the idea. Start with a rough drawing or character concept in seconds.
  • 2 Add motion. Introduce frames, zooms, and subtle tweaks to create instant animation.
  • 3 Refine the vector. Adjust shapes and details precisely while keeping everything reusable.
  • 4 Expand the system. Chain assets and scenes together into richer compositions and product-ready visuals.
